Biography

Daniel Aguado H. (27) is a Geographer Engineer from Peru, trained in the classrooms of San Marcos University with experience in the field of Geomatics. He currently works at the Geological, Mining and Metallurgical Institute of Peru - INGEMMET, performing process automation and publishing geoprocessing services using the Python programming language applied to Geographic Information Systems. He participated in the development of various applications in the National Institute of Statistics and Informatics of Peru (INEI), as the algorithm of “Automated Segmentation in the Rural Area” for the Population and Housing Censuses 2017, as well as participate in the project “Strengthening Of Capacities at the Local Level “, as part of the technical team, proposing the cartographic standardization of the municipalities of Peru.
